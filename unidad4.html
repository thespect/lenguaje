4.1 Pedagogical Implications

1. Understand Acquisition

Teachers must balance implicit (natural) and explicit (studied) processes. Plan activities: Input ‚Üí Comprehension ‚Üí Production.

2. Adapt to Students

  • Children: Games, songs.
  • Teens: Relevance to identity.
  • Adults: Explicit goals and explanations.

3. Input, Interaction & Feedback

Use visual aids. Provide opportunities for interaction (pairs/groups). Offer immediate, non-punitive feedback.
