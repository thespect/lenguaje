3. The Role of the Environment

The family is the childâ€™s primary linguistic environment. Important factors:

  • Quantity of input: More conversational interaction generally leads to larger vocabulary and more complex grammar.
  • Quality of input: Rich vocabulary, explanations, and reading aloud stimulate stronger language growth.
